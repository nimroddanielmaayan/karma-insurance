- **Array Output (Crucial):** Construct your reply according to the Input Schema of the Structured Output Parser.

For example, Response 1 is the intro, Response 2 is the main point, Response 3 is the follow-up question (1 to 3 response max). Because of this, do not use line breaks (`\n`); just let the array separate the thoughts. If your answer is short and simple, which it should be most of the times, place it all in just one response.
